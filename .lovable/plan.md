
# Joy Pasto – Professional Portfolio Site

A clean, professional single-page static site to help Joy land her next customer service / cashier role. Designed for easy deployment to Netlify or Cloudflare Pages.

## Design
- Clean, professional style with neutral tones (navy/slate + white)
- Clear typography, well-spaced sections, subtle section dividers
- Fully responsive (mobile-friendly)

## Sections (top to bottom)

### 1. Hero
- Joy's name, title ("Customer Service Professional"), and location (Tulsa/Broken Arrow, OK)
- Brief tagline from her professional summary
- Phone number and email displayed prominently

### 2. About
- Expanded professional summary highlighting 15+ years of experience, reliability, and work ethic
- Mention she's seeking part-time mid-day shifts

### 3. Core Competencies / Skills
- Visual cards or badges showcasing: POS Operations, Payment Processing, Customer Service, Reliability, Adaptability
- Brief description under each

### 4. Professional Experience
- Timeline-style layout for her work history:
  - **Big Lots** (June 2008 – Feb 2025) – 16+ years, key accomplishments
  - **Dollar Tree** (Nov 2017 – May 2018) – fast-paced retail experience

### 5. Testimonials / References
- Placeholder section with space for quotes from past employers or colleagues
- "References available upon request" if no quotes are provided yet

### 6. Contact / Footer
- Phone number and email displayed clearly
- Location info
- Simple footer with copyright

## Technical Notes
- Pure static site (no backend needed) – ready for Netlify/CF Pages deployment
- Single-page with smooth scroll navigation
- Fixed top navigation bar with anchor links to each section
